About Us

Since opening our doors in 2020, My Food has been at the forefront of the third wave coffee movement that has taken Indonesia by storm and since then we have shared hundreds of beautiful coffees with thousands of passionate customers. Our mission stayed the same that is to express our passion for Indonesian Arabica Beans and to make it accessible, appreciated and easy to enjoy.

For My Food, Pizza is the sum of culture, history and utmost passion. We admire coffee in all its forms – whole bean, brewed and espresso and we believe that everyone deserves to experience it. That is why we source, roast and sell what we believe is among the finest.
